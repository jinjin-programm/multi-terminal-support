Support

Use the public issue tracker for reproducible product questions and bugs.

Before opening an issue

  1. Confirm that you are using the Direct product and record its version.
  2. Record your macOS version and the smallest steps that reproduce the problem.
  3. Remove tokens, credentials, private paths, private terminal output, and personal information.
  4. Describe what you expected and what happened instead.

Open a public support issue

Security: do not disclose a suspected vulnerability publicly. Use GitHub private vulnerability reporting.

Command-line integrations

When a problem involves a CLI or external service, include its name and version. Do not include its credentials. That tool or service may also require a report through its own support channel.
