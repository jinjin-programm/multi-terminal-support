Privacy

Multi-Terminal Direct is a local-first desktop product. Workspace, session, history, and settings data are kept on the Mac unless you choose to export, copy, synchronize, or transmit them with another tool.

Terminal and tool activity

Commands run in a terminal can read files, start processes, and use the network with your macOS permissions. CLIs and services that you configure may send data under their own settings and privacy terms. Multi-Terminal does not control those third-party destinations.

Distribution and billing

The Direct product is distributed outside the Mac App Store and does not use StoreKit or App Store billing.

Support pages and issues

No analytics script or support form has been added to these static pages. The pages and public issue tracker are hosted by GitHub, whose own terms and privacy practices apply. Anything posted to a public issue is public, so remove secrets and private content first.

Your choices

You choose which workspaces to open, which commands to run, which external CLIs or services to configure, and what information to publish in a support issue.
