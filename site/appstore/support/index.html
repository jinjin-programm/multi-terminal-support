Support

Use the public issue tracker for reproducible app questions. Apple manages billing records and subscription transactions.

Before opening an issue

  1. Record the app version shown by the App Store product and your macOS version.
  2. For folder access, state whether you selected the folder again when prompted.
  3. For purchases, state whether Purchase or Restore Purchases was used. Do not post receipts or account details.
  4. Remove tokens, credentials, private paths, terminal output, and personal information.

Open a public support issue

Security: do not disclose a suspected vulnerability publicly. Use GitHub private vulnerability reporting.

App Store billing

Purchase processing, payment methods, and subscription management are provided by Apple. Use the App Store's subscription management controls for billing changes.
